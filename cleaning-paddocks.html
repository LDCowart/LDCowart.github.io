Steps to Clean Horse Paddocks

  1. Remove any manure and waste materials.
  2. Check for debris and remove any hazardous items.
  3. Ensure that the fencing is secure.
  4. Rotate the grazing areas if possible.
  5. Maintain water sources clean.
