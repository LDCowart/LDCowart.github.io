About Landon Cowart

All the nitty-griity details of our ministry

Evangelism

My Mission is to spread the gospel here at home in my community, and I will accomplish this with the Help of the Holy spirit.

Theology

I was raised as a pentecostal, and am a member of the Assemblies Of God Pentecostal Church, however I attend a Southern Baptist Convention (SBC) Church. My Beliefs are a mixture of this, however i do believe in the Gifts of the Holy spirit including the Evidence of Speaking in tongues and the interpretation of those tongues (Acts 2:1–4).

Community

I Currently reside in Jackson Georgia, therefore my mission field will be primarily Butts County. However I am also Tied to the Encounter Life Church Newnan, which I will be doing Missions in Coweta, fayette, spalding, and surrounding Counties.
