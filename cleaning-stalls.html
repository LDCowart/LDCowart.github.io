Steps to Clean Horse Stalls

  1. Remove the horse from the stall.
  2. Clear out all bedding and waste material.
  3. Scrub the stall walls and floor with disinfectant.
  4. Rinse and allow to dry.
  5. Replace with fresh bedding.
